FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our painting services.

How much does it cost to paint a house in Hudson, MA?

The cost to paint a house in Hudson varies based on size, condition, and paint quality. Interior painting typically ranges from $2-$6 per square foot, while exterior painting ranges from $3-$7 per square foot. Contact us for a free, detailed estimate tailored to your specific project.

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, A&M Painter Inc is fully licensed and insured with $2M in liability coverage. We carry both general liability and workers' compensation insurance to protect you and your property.

How long does a typical painting project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope. A single room typically takes 1-2 days, a full interior 3-5 days, and exterior painting 4-7 days depending on size and weather conditions. We provide accurate timelines during your free estimate.

What type of paint do you use?
We use premium paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and PPG. We select the best paint for each project based on surface type, location (interior/exterior), and your preferences for finish and durability.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all painting and remodeling projects. Call us at (508) 561-6729 or fill out our contact form to schedule your free consultation.

What areas do you serve?
We serve Hudson and surrounding communities including Marlborough, Worcester, Framingham, Natick, Sudbury, Westborough, Northborough, Shrewsbury, and many more towns throughout Massachusetts. Contact us to confirm service in your area.
